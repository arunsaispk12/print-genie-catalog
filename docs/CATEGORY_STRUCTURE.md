# Print Genie - Category & Subcategory Structure

## Overview
This document defines the complete category hierarchy for Print Genie's 3D printing business catalog.

---

## 1. CUSTOM PRINTS (CP)
**Description:** Made-to-order items from customer-provided 3D files

### Subcategories:
- **CP-01: Functional Parts** - Replacement parts, mechanical components, tools
- **CP-02: Decorative Items** - Custom decor, artistic pieces, personalized gifts
- **CP-03: Prototypes** - Single iteration prototypes, proof-of-concept models
- **CP-04: Miniatures & Models** - Custom figurines, scale models, gaming pieces
- **CP-05: Architectural Models** - Building models, landscape designs
- **CP-06: Educational Models** - Teaching aids, scientific models, anatomical prints

---

## 2. PRE-DESIGNED PRODUCTS (PD)
**Description:** Ready-made designs available for immediate purchase

### Subcategories:

#### 2.1 HOME & LIVING (PD-HL)
- **PD-HL-01: Organization & Storage** - Drawer organizers, cable management, hooks
- **PD-HL-02: Kitchen & Dining** - Utensil holders, coasters, cookie cutters
- **PD-HL-03: Bathroom Accessories** - Toothbrush holders, soap dishes, organizers
- **PD-HL-04: Home Decor** - Vases, planters, wall art, picture frames
- **PD-HL-05: Lighting** - Lamp shades, LED diffusers, decorative lights

#### 2.2 TOYS & GAMES (PD-TG)
- **PD-TG-01: Action Figures & Collectibles** - Character figures, display pieces
- **PD-TG-02: Puzzles & Brain Teasers** - 3D puzzles, mechanical puzzles
- **PD-TG-03: Educational Toys** - STEM toys, learning aids
- **PD-TG-04: Board Game Accessories** - Dice towers, card holders, tokens
- **PD-TG-05: Outdoor Toys** - Water toys, flying toys

#### 2.3 TECH & GADGETS (PD-TG)
- **PD-TC-01: Phone & Tablet Accessories** - Stands, cases, grips
- **PD-TC-02: Computer Accessories** - Cable clips, keyboard wrist rests, monitor stands
- **PD-TC-03: Gaming Accessories** - Controller stands, headset holders
- **PD-TC-04: Smart Home Mounts** - Speaker mounts, camera mounts, sensor brackets
- **PD-TC-05: Tool & Equipment Holders** - Drill organizers, soldering iron stands

#### 2.4 AUTOMOTIVE (PD-AU)
- **PD-AU-01: Interior Accessories** - Cup holders, phone mounts, organizers
- **PD-AU-02: Exterior Accessories** - Emblems, trim pieces, custom badges
- **PD-AU-03: Tool Holders & Organizers** - Socket organizers, tool trays
- **PD-AU-04: Replacement Parts** - Knobs, clips, brackets

#### 2.5 JEWELRY & FASHION (PD-JF)
- **PD-JF-01: Rings & Bracelets** - Fashion jewelry, custom bands
- **PD-JF-02: Pendants & Necklaces** - Decorative pendants, custom designs
- **PD-JF-03: Earrings** - Geometric, artistic, personalized
- **PD-JF-04: Brooches & Pins** - Decorative pins, custom badges
- **PD-JF-05: Accessories** - Hair clips, buttons, cufflinks

#### 2.6 OFFICE & STATIONERY (PD-OF)
- **PD-OF-01: Desk Organization** - Pen holders, desk trays, organizers
- **PD-OF-02: Writing Accessories** - Pen stands, business card holders
- **PD-OF-03: Bookmarks & Clips** - Decorative bookmarks, paper clips
- **PD-OF-04: Name Plates & Signs** - Desk name plates, door signs

---

## 3. PROTOTYPING SERVICES (PS)
**Description:** Professional prototyping and iteration services for businesses

### Subcategories:
- **PS-01: Rapid Prototyping** - Fast turnaround single prototypes
- **PS-02: Iterative Development** - Multiple revision cycles
- **PS-03: Functional Testing** - Prototypes for mechanical testing
- **PS-04: Visual/Presentation Models** - High-quality finish for presentations
- **PS-05: Low-Volume Production** - Small batch manufacturing (5-100 units)
- **PS-06: Design Consultation** - Design review and optimization services

---

## 4. MATERIALS & FILAMENTS (MF)
**Description:** 3D printing materials and supplies

### Subcategories:

#### 4.1 FILAMENTS BY TYPE (MF-FT)
- **MF-FT-01: PLA Filament** - Standard, PLA+, specialty PLA
- **MF-FT-02: PETG Filament** - Clear, colored, specialty PETG
- **MF-FT-03: ABS Filament** - Standard ABS, high-impact
- **MF-FT-04: TPU/Flexible** - Soft, flexible filaments
- **MF-FT-05: Specialty Filaments** - Wood-fill, metal-fill, glow-in-dark, color-changing
- **MF-FT-06: Engineering Filaments** - Nylon, PC, carbon fiber composites

#### 4.2 FILAMENTS BY COLOR (MF-FC)
- **MF-FC-01: Solid Colors** - Single color spools
- **MF-FC-02: Multi-Color/Gradient** - Color-changing, rainbow filaments
- **MF-FC-03: Metallic & Shimmer** - Metallic finish, glitter filaments
- **MF-FC-04: Translucent & Clear** - See-through materials

#### 4.3 ACCESSORIES & SUPPLIES (MF-AS)
- **MF-AS-01: Build Surface** - Print beds, build plates, adhesives
- **MF-AS-02: Maintenance Tools** - Nozzles, cleaning tools, lubricants
- **MF-AS-03: Post-Processing** - Sanding tools, paints, finishing supplies
- **MF-AS-04: Storage Solutions** - Filament storage, desiccant, dry boxes

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Always assign** products to the most specific subcategory available
2. **Use primary category** when a product spans multiple subcategories
3. **Update this document** when adding new categories (version control)
4. **Maintain consistency** in category naming conventions
